Bulbuls
Bulbuls are a cheerful bird species with upbeat calls. There is a large family with a wide range of different bulbuls, some being very elegant. They have a large and wide diet, generally taking fruits and insects. Most are monogamous, some appear to mating for life. Some have beautiful calls that have unfortunately resulted in them being captured for the pet trade. They are generally shades of brown but a few species are brightly coloured.
